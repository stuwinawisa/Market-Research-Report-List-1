<p><p>Aminated graphene is available primarily in two market types: black powder and white powder. Black powder, rich in functional amine groups, is favored for its high conductivity and enhanced mechanical properties, making it ideal for applications in electronics and composites. White powder, on the other hand, features less graphene oxide and offers improved dispersibility in solvents and polymers, making it suitable for coatings and energy storage solutions. Both types are integral to advancing materials science and nanotechnology.</p></p>
