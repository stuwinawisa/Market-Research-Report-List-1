<p><strong>The Tyre Vulcanizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Vulcanizer</li><li>Hydraulic Vulcanizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tyre vulcanizer market is primarily segmented into mechanical and hydraulic vulcanizers. Mechanical vulcanizers utilize a system of levers and screws to apply pressure and heat, suitable for smaller-scale operations and repairs. In contrast, hydraulic vulcanizers employ hydraulic systems to exert higher pressures, enabling more efficient and uniform curing, making them ideal for larger production environments. Both types cater to different needs in the tyre repair and manufacturing sectors, enhancing durability and performance of tyres.</p></p>
