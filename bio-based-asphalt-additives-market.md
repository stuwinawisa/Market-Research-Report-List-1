<p><p>The bio-based asphalt additives market encompasses several categories aimed at enhancing asphalt performance. Asphalt modifiers improve flexibility and durability, while antistripping agents enhance adhesion between asphalt and aggregates, reducing moisture damage. Asphalt emulsifiers facilitate the mixing of asphalt with water for easier application. Surfactant additives aid in reducing surface tension, promoting better blending. Foam stabilizers ensure the stability of asphalt foams used in warm mix applications. Other additives may include bio-based fillers or stabilizers that further enhance performance and sustainability.</p></p>
