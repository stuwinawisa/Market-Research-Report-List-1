<p><p>The polyurethane foams market is categorized into three main types: flexible foam, rigid foam, and spray foam. Flexible foam is soft and elastic, commonly used in furniture, bedding, and automotive interiors for comfort. Rigid foam is denser and primarily employed in insulation applications, providing thermal resistance in buildings and appliances. Spray foam is versatile and expands on application, sealing gaps and enhancing insulation in construction, offering energy efficiency and moisture control. Each type serves distinct functions in various industries.</p></p>
