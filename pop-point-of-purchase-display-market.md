<p><p>Point of Purchase (POP) displays come in several market types, each serving unique promotional purposes. Floor displays are large, standalone units placed in retail aisles, designed to capture customer attention. Pallet displays utilize a pallet structure for bulk items, encouraging high visibility and easy access. End-cap displays are positioned at the ends of aisles, strategically highlighting products and promotions. Countertop displays are smaller units placed at checkout counters to tempt impulse buys, maximizing sales potential in high-traffic areas.</p></p>
