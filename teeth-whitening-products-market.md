<p><p>The teeth whitening products market encompasses various types, primarily categorized into surface whiteners and bleaches. Surface whiteners include toothpaste and strips that remove stains through abrasion or mild chemical agents, enhancing the brightness of teeth without altering their inherent color. In contrast, bleaches utilize stronger chemicals, like hydrogen peroxide or carbamide peroxide, to penetrate enamel and lighten the underlying tooth shade effectively. Both product types cater to different consumer needs, offering varying degrees of whitening results.</p></p>
