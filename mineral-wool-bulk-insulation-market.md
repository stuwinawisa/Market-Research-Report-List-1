<p><p>The mineral wool bulk insulation market consists of two primary types: light density and heavy density mineral wool. Light density mineral wool is designed for thermal insulation in residential and commercial applications, offering sound absorption and fire resistance. It is lightweight and easy to handle, making it ideal for walls and ceilings. In contrast, heavy density mineral wool provides enhanced thermal performance and acoustic properties, often used in industrial applications and for soundproofing in high-traffic areas, due to its superior density and durability.</p></p>
