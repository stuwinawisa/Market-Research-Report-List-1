<p><p>The microscopes market is primarily divided into two main types: optical microscopes and electron microscopes. Optical microscopes use visible light and lenses to magnify specimens, making them ideal for biological studies and material inspection. In contrast, electron microscopes utilize electron beams to achieve much higher resolution, allowing for detailed imaging at the atomic level, making them essential in materials science and nanotechnology. Both types serve crucial roles in research, industry, and education, catering to different analytical needs.</p></p>
