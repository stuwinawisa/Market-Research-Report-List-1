<p><strong>The Pitch Propeller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Controllable Pitch Propeller</li><li>Fixed Pitch Propeller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pitch propeller market comprises two primary types: controllable pitch propellers (CPP) and fixed pitch propellers (FPP). Controllable pitch propellers allow for the adjustment of blade angles, enhancing maneuverability and efficiency across various operating conditions, making them suitable for vessels requiring varied speed and thrust. In contrast, fixed pitch propellers have blades set at a constant angle, offering simplicity and lower maintenance, commonly used in vessels with consistent operational profiles. Both types serve specific marine applications based on performance needs.</p></p>
