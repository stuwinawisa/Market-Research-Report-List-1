<p><p>Hydroprocessing catalysts (HPC) are essential in refining processes, primarily used for hydrotreating and hydrocracking hydrocarbons. The market is divided into two main types: transition metal-based catalysts and noble metal-based catalysts. Transition metal-based catalysts, such as those with cobalt, molybdenum, or nickel, are cost-effective and widely used. In contrast, noble metal-based catalysts, including platinum and palladium, offer higher activity and selectivity but at a higher cost. Both types enhance fuel quality and meet stringent environmental regulations in the refining sector.</p></p>
