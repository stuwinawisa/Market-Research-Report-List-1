<p><strong>The Deaerator Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tray-type Deaerator</li><li>Spray-type Deaerator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Deaerator systems are essential in steam generation and water treatment, designed to remove dissolved gases, primarily oxygen and carbon dioxide, from water. Tray-type deaerators utilize multiple trays to enhance the contact between steam and water, promoting efficient gas removal. In contrast, spray-type deaerators disperse the water into fine droplets, exposing a larger surface area to steam, resulting in rapid deaeration. Both types are crucial in preventing corrosion and improving the efficiency of industrial processes.</p></p>
