<p><p>The CV axle joint market is segmented into outboard and inboard joints, each serving distinct functions in vehicle drivetrains. Outboard joints connect the axle to the wheel hub, allowing for smooth rotation during steering and suspension movement. In contrast, inboard joints link the axle to the transmission, accommodating changes in angle while transmitting power. This segmentation is pivotal as each type addresses different mechanical demands, impacting performance, durability, and compatibility in various vehicle applications.</p></p>
