<p><p>The mechanical power transmission market consists of several types, each serving unique applications. Gear drives utilize interlocking gears to transmit power efficiently with high torque. Chain drives employ a chain and sprockets for reliable power transfer, commonly found in motorcycles and bicycles. Hydraulic transmission uses fluid power to transmit energy, ideal for heavy machinery due to its ability to handle high loads. Other market types include belt drives and friction drives, expanding the range of solutions for various industrial needs.</p></p>
