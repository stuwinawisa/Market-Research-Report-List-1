<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The DC Centrifugal Fans Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Forward-curved</li><li>Backward-curved</li><li>Straight Radial</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DC centrifugal fans are categorized into three main types based on their design and airflow characteristics. Forward-curved fans have blades that curve in the direction of rotation, offering high airflow and low pressure. Backward-curved fans feature blades angled opposite to the rotation, providing efficient performance and high pressure, ideal for challenging systems. Straight radial fans have blades that extend directly outward, generating a strong airflow but lower pressure, suitable for applications requiring robust air movement without significant resistance. Each type serves specific industrial needs.</p></p>
