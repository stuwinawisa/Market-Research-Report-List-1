<p><p>Nickel powder for multilayer ceramic capacitors (MLCC) is primarily categorized into two types: nano nickel powder and sub-micron nickel powder. Nano nickel powder consists of extremely fine particles, offering high surface area and improved conductivity, making it ideal for high-performance electronics. In contrast, sub-micron nickel powder features slightly larger particles, balancing conductivity and mechanical strength. Both types are essential in enhancing the efficiency and reliability of MLCCs, catering to the growing demand in consumer electronics and advanced manufacturing applications.</p></p>
