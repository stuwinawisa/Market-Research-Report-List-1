<p><ul><li>Bare type</li><li>Mosaic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chilled beams are advanced cooling systems used in commercial buildings, categorized mainly into two market types: bare type and mosaic type. Bare chilled beams function independently, circulating chilled water to cool indoor spaces without additional equipment. In contrast, mosaic chilled beams integrate multiple components, such as lighting and ventilation, into a cohesive unit, enhancing aesthetic appeal and energy efficiency. Both types offer sustainable climate control solutions, but their application depends on design preferences and specific building requirements.</p></p>
