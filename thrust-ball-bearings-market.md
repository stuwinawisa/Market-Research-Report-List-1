<p><p>Thrust ball bearings are specialized bearing types designed to handle axial loads. The market is segmented into single-direction and double-direction bearings. Single-direction bearings accommodate thrust loads in one direction, making them suitable for applications like gear drives. In contrast, double-direction bearings support axial loads in both directions, ideal for systems where reverse motion is possible, such as in certain rotary applications. The choice between these types depends on the specific operational requirements and load conditions of the machinery involved.</p></p>
