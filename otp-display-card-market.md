<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The OTP Display Card Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Event-based (HOTP) Tokens</li><li>Time-based (TOTP) Tokens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OTP display card market comprises two main types: event-based (HOTP) tokens and time-based (TOTP) tokens. HOTP tokens generate one-time passwords based on a counter that increments with each use, making them ideal for systems requiring sporadic authentication. In contrast, TOTP tokens produce passwords that are valid for a specific time interval, typically 30 seconds, enhancing security for frequent logins. Both token types bolster authentication processes across industries, reducing fraud and improving user data protection.</p></p>
