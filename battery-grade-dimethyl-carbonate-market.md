<p><p>The Battery Grade Dimethyl Carbonate (DMC) market comprises two primary production methods: the Propylene Oxide Method and the Ethylene Oxide Method. The Propylene Oxide Method involves the reaction of propylene oxide with carbon dioxide, yielding high-purity DMC suitable for battery applications. In contrast, the Ethylene Oxide Method utilizes ethylene oxide as a feedstock, providing another route to produce battery-grade DMC. These methods cater to the increasing demand for high-performance electrolytes in lithium-ion batteries, critical for energy storage solutions.</p></p>
