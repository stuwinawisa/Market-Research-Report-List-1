<p><ul><li>On-Premise</li><li>Cloud</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dealer management systems (DMS) can be categorized into two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise systems are installed locally on a dealer's hardware, offering greater control over data security and customization but requiring significant IT maintenance. Conversely, cloud-based systems are hosted on remote servers, providing easier access, automatic updates, and lower upfront costs. They enhance collaboration and scalability, allowing dealers to manage operations effectively from any location with internet access, thus catering to varying business needs.</p></p>
