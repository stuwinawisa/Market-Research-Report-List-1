<p><strong>The Continuous Wave Quantum Cascade Laser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fabry–Perot</li><li>Distributed Feedback (DFB)</li><li>Tunable External Cavities</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Continuous Wave Quantum Cascade Laser market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Fabry–Perot Lasers**: Characterized by a simple cavity design, these lasers offer broad spectral output but lower wavelength stability.</p><p>2. **Distributed Feedback (DFB) Lasers**: These provide single-mode operation with enhanced wavelength stability, ideal for applications requiring precise tuning.</p><p>3. **Tunable External Cavities**: Incorporating an external grating, these lasers allow for adjustable wavelengths, making them suitable for a variety of scientific and industrial applications. Each type caters to specific needs in precision, tunability, and application suitability.</p></p>
