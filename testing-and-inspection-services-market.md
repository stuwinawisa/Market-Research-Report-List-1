<p><strong>The Testing and Inspection Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Initial Production Check (IPC)</li><li>During Production Check (DUPRO)</li><li>Final Random Inspection (FRI)</li><li>Loading/Unloading Supervision (LS/US)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Testing and Inspection Services market encompasses various types of quality control measures to ensure product standards. Initial Production Check (IPC) assesses samples before mass production begins. During Production Check (DUPRO) evaluates ongoing operations to identify issues while production is active. Final Random Inspection (FRI) occurs before shipment, ensuring the entire batch meets quality expectations. Loading/Unloading Supervision (LS/US) monitors product handling during transport to prevent damage. Other services may include laboratory testing or compliance auditing, enhancing overall product reliability.</p></p>
