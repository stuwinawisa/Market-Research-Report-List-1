<p><strong>The MRI-guided Radiation Therapy Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>0.5 T</li><li>1.5 T</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MRI-guided Radiation Therapy Systems can be categorized based on the magnetic field strength, primarily into three types: 0.5 T, 1.5 T, and Other. The 0.5 T systems offer lower field strength, making them more compact and less costly but may provide lower image quality. The 1.5 T systems are more common, delivering high-resolution images for better tumor targeting and treatment planning. "Other" includes emerging technologies and variations in field strength, catering to specific clinical needs and applications in radiation therapy.</p></p>
